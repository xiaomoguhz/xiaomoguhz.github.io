个人简介Biography

我是王俊杰，哈尔滨工业大学（深圳）电子信息博士研究生（2023 年至今），研究方向为多模态理解与生成、多模态视觉感知、以及大模型高效推理。 我曾在字节跳动 TikTok 与快手 Kling 团队担任大模型研究实习生，研究内容覆盖图像/视频生成 Agent、多模态大模型蒸馏与视觉编码器后训练。

I am Wang Junjie, a Ph.D. student in Electronic Information at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen (since 2023). My research focuses on multimodal understanding & generation, multimodal visual perception, and efficient reasoning for large models. I have interned as a research intern at ByteDance (TikTok) and the Kling Team at Kuaishou, working on image/video generation agents, multimodal LLM distillation, and vision-encoder post-training.
