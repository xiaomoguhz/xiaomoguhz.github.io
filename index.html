About

I am Junjie Wang, a Ph.D. student in Electronic Information at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. My research focuses on multimodal understanding & generation, multimodal visual perception, and efficient reasoning for large models. I have interned as a research intern at ByteDance (TikTok) and the Kling Team at Kuaishou, working on image/video generation agents, multimodal LLM distillation, and vision-encoder post-training.

I expect to graduate in 2027 and am on the job market for industrial research positions. If you are interested, feel free to reach out via email or WeChat (xiaomoguhzz).

Experience

ByteDance (TikTok) 2025.09 – 2026.04
Research Intern, Large Model
  • Image-Generation Agent: formalized T2I generation as a Reason-Reflect-Rectify loop; built the R³-Bench benchmark and R³-Refiner RL method (accepted to ICML 2026).
  • TinyMLLM Distillation: built a two-stage distillation pipeline on Qwen3-VL / Qwen3.5; led recipe design, supervision-data construction, teacher training, and 2B-model distillation, deployed in live-streaming business to replace Gemini and cut cost.
Kuaishou, Kling Team 2026.04 – Present
Research Intern, Large Model
  • MLLM Vision-Encoder Post-training: built a three-stage post-training pipeline on MS-Swift with a multi-teacher distillation scheme, aiming at a vision encoder beyond Qwen3.5 ViT with stronger spatial and temporal perception.
  • Video-Generation Agent: post-trained a Qwen3.5-based MLLM agent that calls video-generation models with planning, reflection, and feedback rectification; designed the corresponding benchmark and RL training method.
